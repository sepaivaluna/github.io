HOW TO PLAY


To begin, you and your opponent will both begin with 16 Health and 0 Alcohol level. This is what your meter will look like:

You can see, you have a total of 16 hearts!
You will select a card from your hand. Whatever you choose will impact your opponent directly. For example this could be your card (Hint: hover over it):

Robert Plant Rum

Use this card to give 3 alcohol to your rival!

There are a total of 10 different types of cards that you can potentially choose from!
Once you select your card your turn is over and it's your opponent's turn. The objective of the game is to get your opponents Health and Alcohol level to equal. Once that happens the game ends!
