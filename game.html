HOW TO PLAY


Are you ready to try your luck? Well in this game you can. You and your opponent will both begin with 16 Health. This is what your meter will look like:
You will select a card from your hand. Whatever you choose will impact your opponent directly. For example this could be your card (Hint: hover over it):

Robert Plant Rum

Use this card to give 3 alcohol to your rival!

There are a total of 10 different types of cards that you can potentially get!
Once you select your card your turn is over and it's your opponent's turn. The objective of the game is to get your opponent's Health to zero. Once that happens the game ends!
